# The Donald

## Character definition

A deadpan editorial-cartoon caricature of Donald Trump for satirical concept illustrations. Not a mascot and not a hit piece: the humor lives in the **staging** — the gap between what the labels claim and what the drawing shows — delivered with the house's blank calm. He is a public figure drawn in the register of newspaper cartoons: iconic mannerisms, gentle absurdity, no fabricated harmful claims.

## Form

- Rounded-square head, warm light-tan flat fill.
- **The hair**: one solid golden swoop covering the top, sweeping across the forehead with a flick at the side — the single most identifying shape; get this right and the rest can be minimal.
- Small dot eyes under two short, slightly angled brow lines (serious squint); tiny pursed mouth (short line or a very small oval pout).
- Suit body: white-filled rounded block with two black lapel strokes; **extra-long red tie** down the middle, ending well below the beltline.
- Thin stick limbs per house style; small circle hands when gesturing.
- Outline slightly irregular and hand-drawn, never a perfect vector shape.

## Colors (character identity — exempt from the annotation palette)

- Hair: `#f2c14e` (flat gold)
- Skin: `#f3d3ac` (neutral warm tan)
- Tie: `#c9382e` (same hue as the accent — intentional; the tie is identity, the accent is the claim)
- Suit: white fill, `#111` line

## Personality

- Supremely, serenely confident; the deadpan is bottomless.
- Treats every mundane system like a rally announcement.
- Grades everything; his own things are always ranked first.
- Never doubts, never hurries, never labors — presents.

## Persona (voice & flair)

- **Label voice**: superlative-first — "tremendous", "yuge", "the best", "nobody's ever seen", "everybody says so", "so true". Rivals and inconveniences get "fake" or "SAD!". Restraint rule: at most ONE signature phrase per image and at most one ALL-CAPS word — the rest of the labels stay plain, which is what keeps it deadpan.
- **Signature gestures**: accordion hands (both palms pinching air mid-claim), arms spread wide ("this big"), a firm thumbs-up, pointing into the distance.
- **Signature props**: a podium, the extra-long red tie, gold block letters spelling his brand, an oversized flourish signature, a red cap.
- **Staging quirk**: he never does the labor — he **announces, brands, and ranks** it. The core action is always presentation: unveiling a modest reality under a magnificent claim. The claim goes in the red accent label; the drawing quietly disagrees.

## Action library

- Announcing at a podium (to any audience, however small).
- Affixing gold brand letters onto an object mid-project.
- Unveiling something tiny with maximum ceremony (curtain, ribbon, drape).
- Signing something with an enormous flourish and holding it up.
- Ranking objects with 1st/2nd/3rd tags, his own entry first.
- Measuring something with a tape and reading out a much bigger number.
- Giving a thumbs-up next to a thing that clearly isn't finished.

## Don'ts

- No demeaning physical mockery (no orange-skin gag, no body jokes).
- No fabricated harmful claims, no violence, no election-fraud scenarios — keep to timeless, widely known tropes (wall, crowd size, branding, superlatives, signatures).
- Never more than one shouting label per image.
- Never cute, never grotesque — deadpan editorial-sketch register.

## SVG recipe

- Head: rounded rect (rx ≈ 30) ~100×95 at primary scale on a 1600×900 canvas, fill `#f3d3ac`, stroke `#111` at 3px.
- Hair: one closed gold path (`#f2c14e`, 2.5px `#111` stroke) hugging the skull's top third, dipping across the forehead toward one temple, with a small rounded flick past the head's side edge.
- Face: dot eyes (r ≈ 5) under two 16–20px brow lines angled ~10° inward; mouth = 10px line or 8×5 oval outline.
- Body: white rounded rect ~0.9×head width; lapels = two strokes from the shoulders meeting at mid-chest; tie = red strip (10–14px wide) from the collar to ~15% below the body's bottom edge, small knot square at top.
- Limbs: `#111` stick lines at 5–6px; small circle hands (r ≈ 7) for accordion/thumbs-up gestures.
- Scale: primary ≈ 15–25% of canvas height; the hair swoop must stay legible at any scale.
- The wobble filter supplies irregularity — build clean shapes and let the filter roughen them.

## Image-gen prompt block

A deadpan hand-drawn caricature of Donald Trump: rounded-square head, one solid golden hair swoop with a side flick, small dot eyes under angled brows, tiny pursed mouth, white suit block with two lapel strokes and an extra-long red tie, thin stick limbs. Editorial-cartoon register: calm, serious, slightly absurd — never grotesque, never cute. He must perform the core conceptual action by announcing, branding, or unveiling it, not decorate the scene.

## The test

If removing him leaves the image's core metaphor fully standing, he is too decorative. Rewrite so the announcement/branding/unveiling IS the mechanism of the joke.
