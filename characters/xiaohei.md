# 小黑 (Xiaohei)

## Character definition

小黑 is a small solid-black creature, the fixed visual IP of Ian's Chinese article illustrations. Not a mascot, not a sticker, not cute decoration — an absurd worker earnestly participating in the operation of the system being drawn.

## Form

- Solid black body: rounded bean / egg / blob, filled black.
- Two white dot eyes, no pupils. Expression empty, calm, serious.
- Thin stick legs; occasionally thin stick arms.
- The body may morph into a cylinder, black bean, black box, funnel, shadow, hole mouth, or the dark interior of a machine — keep the white dot eyes visible so it stays 小黑.
- Outline slightly irregular, hand-drawn. No mouth by default.

## Personality

- Very earnest, but the job is slightly absurd.
- Like a low-key system operator.
- Dry humor; never sells cuteness.
- A little clumsy, but not dumb.
- Genuinely responsible for some task inside the whiteboard sketch.

## Action library

- Hauling material.
- Pulling lines to converge information sources.
- Stuck inside a breakpoint.
- Operating a "judgment" lever inside a machine.
- Becoming the sorting funnel itself.
- Cutting up the "material fish".
- Stamping hand-off phrases.
- Leading a hand-off path along.
- Holding a warning sign over a pit.
- Reaching out of a hole but failing to catch what falls.
- Carrying bricks, building bridges, opening doors, sorting, recording on the side.

## Don'ts

- Not an over-cute mascot, not a children's-cartoon character.
- No elaborate clothing, emoji-pack faces, or sparkly eyes.
- Never just standing in a corner watching.
- Never upstaging the structure it explains.
- Not too commercial, too rounded, too polished.

## SVG recipe

- Body: one rounded bean/egg path, fill `#111`, no stroke needed (or a 2px self-colored stroke for silhouette wobble).
- Face: two white circles (r ≈ 6–9 at a 1600×900 canvas), set high and close together. No pupils, no mouth.
- Limbs: 3px black stick lines; arms stretch rubber-hose style when the action needs reach.
- Morphs: funnel/box/hole variants keep the solid-black fill and white dot eyes.
- Scale: primary ≈ 15–25% of canvas height (≈ 140–220px tall); smaller when repeated.
- The wobble filter supplies irregularity — build clean shapes and let the filter roughen them.

## Image-gen prompt block

小黑, a small solid-black absurd creature with white dot eyes, tiny thin legs, blank serious expression, slightly uneven hand-drawn body shape. 小黑 must perform the core conceptual action, not decorate the scene. Make 小黑 serious, deadpan, and slightly bizarre, not cute.

## The test

If removing 小黑 leaves the image's core metaphor fully standing, 小黑 is too decorative. Rewrite so 小黑 performs the action.
